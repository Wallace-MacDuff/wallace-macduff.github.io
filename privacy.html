5. DO WE USE COOKIES AND OTHER TRACKING TECHNOLOGIES?

In Short: We only use cookies to make the farm work better, not to track you across the internet.

Let's put your mind at ease: we do not participate in the ad-tracking, data-scalping, or crooked data broker marketplace. There is nothing smelly on our farm (except Wally, sometimes).

Our website uses a few simple, standard "cookies" (small data files saved to your browser). We only use these for two reasons:

  • Functional Cookies: These help the website remember your choices, like keeping the farm stage "dimmed" if you turn on focus mode, or ensuring the interactive games run smoothly without crashing.
  • Basic Analytics: We use high-level, anonymous analytics just to see what cities or countries our readers are visiting from and which pages are the most popular. It helps us to develop new features and make a better experience for everyone visiting the farm.

We do not use third-party marketing trackers, and we will NEVER follow you around the internet with creepy ads.

10. CHILDREN'S PRIVACY (COPPA)

In Short: We protect children's privacy and comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA).

The farm stage is designed to be a safe, family-friendly environment. We do not knowingly collect, use, or disclose personal information from children under the age of 13 without prior verifiable parental consent. Children under 13 are strictly prohibited from submitting personal data (such as names, locations, or email addresses) to our Guestbook or Reader Map without direct supervision and permission from a parent or guardian. If we learn that we have inadvertently collected personal information from a child under 13 without parental consent, we will take immediate steps to delete that information from our records.
